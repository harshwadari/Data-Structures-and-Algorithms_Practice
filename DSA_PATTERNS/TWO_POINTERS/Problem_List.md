# 🎯 Goal

After completing these 30 problems, you should be comfortable with:

- Opposite Direction Two Pointers
- Slow & Fast Pointers
- Pair Sum Problems
- K-Sum Problems (2Sum, 3Sum, 4Sum)
- Merging Sorted Arrays
- Palindrome & String Problems
- Removing Duplicates
- Array Rearrangement
- Dutch National Flag Algorithm
- Floyd's Cycle Detection (Fast & Slow Pointer)
- Greedy + Two Pointers
- Interval Problems
- Trapping Rain Water
- Advanced Pointer Movement Techniques
